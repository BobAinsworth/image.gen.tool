People Image Generation Prompt Tool

Create amazing one person images!

(No coding or prompting experience necessary)

Make one or more choices and/or type your own wording into the box.

Subject basics:

(1) What is the purpose or point of the image?

Example: “Professional LinkedIn headshot with a friendly, trustworthy feel.”

(2) Who is the intended audience for the image?

(3) What is the age category of the person?

(4) What is the gender presentation?

Face & body:

(5) What is the ethnicity or facial characteristics?

(6) What emotion or expression should the person show?

(7) What facial features should the person have?

(8) Physical-appearance choices

(9) What color hair does the subject have?

(10) What hairstyle does the subject have?

(11) What is the person’s overall stature?

Framing & pose:

(12) What is the camera framing?

(13) What pose or body position should they have?

(14) What clothing style should they wear?

Environment & lighting:

(15) What is the environment or background?

(16) In what direction is the subject looking?

(17) What direction should the lighting come from?

(18) What lighting style should be used?

Style & technical:

(19) What art style or realism level do you want?

(20) What lens style or technical details should apply?

(21) What aspect ratio should the final image use?

Extras & mood:

(22) Any additional elements to include?

(23) Any elements to avoid?

(24) What is the tone or mood of the overall image?

(25) Is there anything else you wish to describe?

Now that you've answered all of the questions, click on the button below to generate a summary you can use as an AI image prompt.
